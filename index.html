BlackJack

In this game there are three buttons 1) Hit , 2)Stand ,3)Deal. this is simpler version of blackjack game where the one making higher points than other, wins. maximum limit of scores are 21. Hit button is for the person playing this game so whenever we press that button one new card will be added. Stand button is for Bot to play whenever we press that button, card will start appearing in the bot side. and whose score is higher will be declared as a winner and the same will also be updated in the sccore table below.
